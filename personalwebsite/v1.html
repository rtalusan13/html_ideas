About Me

Sophomore in Electrical Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. As a kid, I competed and won several math competitions, fueling my passions for complex problem-solving and higher-level math.

Today, these skills have translated to my education in engineering, where I love learning more about computer systems and the foundations of computing, working with both low-level and high-level understandings through programming and circuit design.

Previously, I graduated from the Illinois Mathematics and Science Academy (IMSA) in Aurora, IL, a nationally-ranked STEM-focused school that guided me in pursuing my academic and personal interests.

Outside of education, my hobbies and fascinations include cooking, games of all kinds (sports, chess, video games, etc.), and self-improvement in various aspects of life. I always grew up with a sense of competition, refining my skills under the guise of "Iron sharpens iron."
